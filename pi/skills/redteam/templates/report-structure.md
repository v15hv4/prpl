# Report Structure Template

1. Title, date, assessor/session, scope, exclusions.
2. Executive summary: business impact and highest-risk themes.
3. Coverage: phase status, blocked/skipped reasons, tools missing, account gaps.
4. Critical findings.
5. High findings.
6. Medium findings.
7. Low / informational findings.
8. Candidate targets not probed due to scope rules.
9. Methodology summary.
10. Appendix: output index and redacted evidence references.
